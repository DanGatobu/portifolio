Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Block Puzzle game (hereinafter referred to as "the App") for mobile devices, created by Dan (hereinafter referred to as "we", "us", or "our").

Information Collection and Use

The App is designed with your privacy in mind. We want to be completely transparent about what data we do and do not collect.

Data We DO NOT Collect

The App does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:
  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, advertising ID)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Crash reports
  • In-app behavior or gameplay data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game settings and preferences
  • High scores and game progress
  • Control customization settings

This data never leaves your device and is only used to provide game functionality. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is deleted.

Permissions

The App requests the following permission:

  • VIBRATE: Used to provide haptic feedback during gameplay for enhanced user experience

This permission does not access any personal data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, advertising networks, or external SDKs that could collect your data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App is suitable for all ages and does not contain any content that requires parental consent.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of your personal information being accessed, disclosed, or compromised through the use of this App.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. All game data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the App.
